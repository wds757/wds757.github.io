About

Excited to transition to a full time position so I can focus my energy on the success of one company and collaborate with a great team. Positively embracing change and learning opportunities with a growth mindset!

My Story

Where are you based?

I currently live in Virginia.

How do you spend your free time?

I love spending time outdoors with family - hiking, kayaking and exploring different parks. I also enjoy caring for my indoor plants, playing games, and having fun with friends.

What’s a fun fact about you?

I used to help with wildlife rehabilitation and also volunteered at the zoo. Learning how to care for all the different species was amazing. The best part was getting to pet rhinos and getting to see their different personalities.

What’s your professional experience?

While earning my BS in Graphic Design, the classes I enjoyed the most were psychology based — recognizing how design affects human thoughts and feelings amazed me.

After college, I started as an intern with the Society of Automotive Engineers and worked my way up to be the Aerospace Team with three direct reports. I managed all the projects that came through for our team. One of the projects was the bimonthly Aerospace Engineering magazine. When my husband needed to move for his job, they put me under contract to remotely manage and produce the magazine while working with the editor.

Once in Virginia, this gave me a opportunity to start my own business and Wolfe Design Studio was established. The business was built through networking and referrals. I've had many clients for over 10 years each. As a freelancer, I got to work on numerous types of design projects. I helped companies establish and grow their branding with marketing communication pieces and website design. I've also had the privilege to work with many marketing consultants, writers, and web developers to offer complete agency packages. The favorite part of what I do is having the opportunity to work with a variety of industries and businesses, but mostly getting to help a diverse group of owners grow their businesses to the next level.
